Interests

While I may have not been able to find my career in music, it reamains an important part of my life and I am an active musician in southern and central Minnesota. I perform most often with my brass quartet, The Mostly Conical Brass Quartet, and my New Orleans jazz band, The Bass Brand Brass Band. I even had the opportunity to perform at a Timberwolves halftime show!

I am also a long time fan of the marching arts, and have spent seasons as a marching member with Minnesota Brass Inc, The Madison Scouts, and The Govenaires drum and bugle corps. More recently, I have been trying to share my knowledge with the next generation of performers and have spent 2 seasons as a brass instructor for The Govenaires as well as the Mankato 77 Lancers Marching Band.
